### Algebra B — Summation Formulas
| Theorem Name | Statement |
|--------|------|
| `my_gauss` | Gauss's formula: $\sum_{k=0}^{n} k = \dfrac{n(n+1)}{2}$ |
| `sum_sq` | Sum of squares: $\sum_{k=0}^{n} k^2 = \dfrac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}$ |
| `sum_cubes` | Sum of cubes: $\sum_{k=0}^{n} k^3 = \left(\dfrac{n(n+1)}{2}\right)^2$ |
| `sum_arith` | Sum of an arithmetic sequence |
| `sum_geom` | Sum of a geometric sequence: $(r-1)\sum r^k = r^n-1$ |
| `sum_geom_div` | Closed form of a geometric series ($r \neq 1$) |
